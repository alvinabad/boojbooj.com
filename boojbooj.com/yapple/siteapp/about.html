Company Overview

booj in punjabi (language spoken in North India) means find.

Boojbooj introduces social aspect of watching YouTube videos on the web. We enable users to watch videos with their twitter community. Viewers will be able to communicate and connect with each other through the exchange of comments and answers to one simple question:

Do you find anything interesting in this video?

Users can watch YouTube videos by simply pasting the YouTube URL and clicking on "Watch Video" button. Stay tuned to this blog or follow us on twitter at boojbooj for all the boojbooj news. We are based in Santa Clara, California.
