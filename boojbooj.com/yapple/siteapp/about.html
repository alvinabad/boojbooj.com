About Us

Company Overview

booj in punjabi (language spoken in North India) means find.

We are a privately-held bay area company committed to building the best social experience for users watching YouTube videos with their friends and family. We deliver a simple way for users to watch their favorite YouTube videos, and communicate and connect with each other through the exchange of comments.

Team

Boojbooj team is comprised of Carnegie Mellon University faculty and graduates who bring a breadth of experience building highly scalable and fault tolerant systems. The team comes from such well-known companies as Business Objects, March first and Charles Schwab.
